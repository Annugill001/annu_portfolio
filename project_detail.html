Gamify

Gamified Stress-Free EdTech Ecosystem

The Big Idea

Students target core academic anxiety everyday. QuestStudy enters as a hybrid framework that redesigns tedious syllabus trackers into gamified daily quests.

objective


To reduce academic pressure through psychological positive reinforcement & to make study interesting with the help of games and task

Core Systems Built

🌐 Dual Mechanics (Online + Offline)

Online mode: Students compete in quiz battles and complete short digital tasks, with progress synced in real time.

Offline mode: The app automatically runs Pomodoro study sessions and tracks time spent studying at a desk or in the library, helping students stay focused without needing to use screens constantly.

🪙 Task Economy (Game Coins)

Completing dynamic practice sets, mock exams, or consistent study targets rewards users with proprietary profile coins. These coins act as tokens to customize dashboard tiers or trade for real student perks.

🧠 Stress-Free Interface Layout

Designed with a calm, dark-themed interface to reduce mental fatigue and help students stay focused. Instead of stressful performance scores or rankings, progress is represented through character growth and level-up mechanics, making learning feel rewarding rather than pressuring.

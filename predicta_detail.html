Predicta

An intelligent system that predicts student performace based on time table,attendence and study habits.

Overview

Predicta is an system designed to empower students by helping them analyze their academic performace. By combining predictive analysis with critical time management tools, it keeps the record of student actions and help them to maintain good timetable

Key Features

  • Performance Prediction: Analyzes test scores and study trends to forecast final academic outcomes.
  • Time Management Framework: Integrated schedulers to allocate daily study hours effectively.
  • Attendance Analytics: Seamlessly logs and monitors the exact number of classes attended to check eligibility margins.
  • Attendance Analytics: calculate your GPA
  • Risk Alert Trigger: Flags warning prompts if study hours or attendance fall below safe thresholds.
